Take a look at our services

Regardless of industry or specific staff challenge, Sirenum helps organisations gain efficiencies across the entire staff management lifecycle. Learn more about how Sirenum modules optimise each step along the way by clicking on the lifecycle to the left. To see Sirenum in action, simply complete the form below and one of our representatives will be in touch to schedule a demo of our platform.

Onboarding

Integrates with leading ATS platforms to facilitate onboarding of staff, simplifying access to crucial information downstream.

Mobile Apps

Calendar management. Shift approval and trading. Holiday requests. MySirenum gives you and your teams schedule management at your fingertips.

Financial

Ensure correct payroll data, automate invoice development, and manage associated budgets, freeing management to focus on higher-value activities.

Monitoring

Know where your staff is, capture time and attendance, anticipate problems with staff, manage patrol and lone workers, control health and safety, and more.

Analytics

Easily access the most advanced reporting, analytics, and dashboarding capabilities on the market to make more informed staff management decisions.

Human Resources

Keep your mission critical employee information accurate, updated, and accessible, while addressing staff-specific requirements.

Compliance

Manage staff certifications, licenses, and permits to ensure a robust health and safety programme and improved operations.

Scheduling

Ensure efficient and effective operations with the right person with the right skills at the right place at the right time.
